23. tolong saya buatlah 5 contoh deskripsi hewan buas bahada Inggris


JAGUAR

Jaguar is the largest cat in the Americas. They have a muscular body, a strong jaw, and a broad head. Their entire body is covered by yellow and brown hair.

They are carnivores and are one of the most feared predators by herbivorous animals. They are also have a value to human culture. These animals often have a central role in the story, songs, and prayers of the American people. However, today Jaguar has completely disappeared from America. They become rare animals and are prohibited to be hunted.

As carnivores, they eat anything that can be caught. Deer, crocodiles, snakes, turtles, eggs, frogs, fish, and monkeys are the variation of the animals they catch.

In the worldwide, jaguar populations are so little. Currently they are probably only about 15,000.

Elephant

Elephant is a herbivor animal and eat all almost all of the vegetations and fruits. They have almost hairless skin, wide ears, four legs, and long trunk. Elephants is known as the largest mammals. The elephants also have very sharp auditory because they have big and wide ears. By these ears, they can hear sounds from a distance about tens kilometer. At the back of the ears, they have one of the softest parts of their body which is called the knuckle. In a zoo, Mahouts (elephant trainers) use their feet to steer or give commands to the animal via tha knuckle. Now, they are a kind of protected animals because their population remains little in their habitat. Their population began to decline year by year.

Giraffe 

Giraffes are the tallest mammals in the world. We can find them in central, eastern and southern Africa. Some of them are also kept in the zoo. A giraffe has a long neck and long legs. The long neck helps it to eat leaves from the tall trees. It pulls the leaves by its long tounge. Around his body, it has spotted patterns. “Giraffa camelopardalis” is its scientific name. On the top of its head, there are small “horns” or knobs. They are used to protect the head when fighting.

Billy : No. What's wrong? ( Tidak. Ada apa?)

Rose : She was running away when we saw a crocodile near the river. (Dia kabur saat kami melihat buaya di sungai)

Billy : What? A crocodile? What were you doing there? (Apa? Buaya? Apa yang kalian lakukan di sana?)

Rose : We looked for my uncle. He said that he would fishing there. (Kami mencari pamanku. Dia bilang dia akan memancing di sana)

Billy : Is a crocodile big? (Apa buayanya besar?)

Rose : Not too. But, its eyes so creepy. And its body so rough. And one thing that very makes me scare. Its teeth is so sharp. (Tidak terlalu. Tapi, matanya sangat menyeramkan. Dan tubuhnya sangat kasar. Dan satu hal yang sangat membuatku takut. Giginya sangat tajam)
