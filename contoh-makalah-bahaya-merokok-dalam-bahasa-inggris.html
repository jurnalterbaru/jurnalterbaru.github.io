20. buatlah 1 paragraf pidato bahasa inggris tentang bahaya narkoba/bahaya merokok​


Jawaban:

Assalamu’alaikum wr.wb

There are no words worthy saying but uttering all the praise and gratitude to Allah Swt because of all of His blessing, mercy, and guidance upon all of us that make us still able to take any advantage of his blissful blessing, namely our health and the oxygen we breathe in without needing to cost anything.

Shalawat and salam may everlastingly be upon our adoration, the Great Prophet Muhammad Saw who have led us from pitch black darkness into the shining bright path.

Today is world’s cigarette-free day. I wish everyone who’s attending here has understood well about how dangerous the cigarette’s effects are for health.

In our country, the rules of the regulation laws for cigarettes or cigarette prohibitions such as information about infertility and impotence that only appear to become sweetening words. If we see other countries which are seen to be free nations, such as the state of New York in USA, it has been enforcing the regulation laws for cigarettes and the heavy sanctions.

The prohibitions to smoke in public places like around office areas, malls, and others, have been enforced. The businesses of restaurants or bars which are seen not following this new rule will be fined by US $ 2,000 which is not a little amount of fine. We are supposed to learn from them if we recall of how dangerous the cigarettes for our health.

To all the audience that I proud of,

cigarette is a poisonous thing that gives relaxation and suggestion effect to make you feel more manly. Behind of that small benefit of cigarette, it is contained a great danger for those who smoke it or people around the smokers who actually are not smokers.

The smoke of cigarette contains more or less than 4000 chemical substances which 200 of them are poisonous and toxic, and 43 others can cause cancer for your body. Some of those dangerous substances include tar, nicotine, carbon monoxide, etc.Smoke that comes out from a just stubbed-out cigarette on ashtray contains three times of cancer-triggering substances in the air and 50 times of eyes and respiration-irritating substances. The shorter cigarette the higher of toxins contained that are ready to fly around the air. A place that’s covered with cigarette smoke pollution is a more dangerous place than pollution you can find in busy jammed traffic.Someone who tries to smoke usually will get addicted since cigarette is addictive that’s hard to stop in any condition. A heavy smoker will prefer smoking to eating foods if he/she has a limited amount of money.A high-priced cigarette will really trouble people who are rated as poor people so that the money they are supposed to use for family’s prosperity and health is frequently shifted to buy cigarettes. Cigarettes with popular brands are usually owned by some foreign cigarette enterprises that come from overseas. It makes some amount of money spent by the smokers will be transferred to foreign countries that cuts the amount of domestic exchange. Cigarette factories that employ many workers will not be able to increase their workers’ living standards so that if the factories are closed then the workers could be employed in other working places which are more creative and conducive to much domestic exchange.Some of smokers generally will invite other people who don’t smoke to smoke so that they will feel the same suffering, namely being trapped in a great addiction of evil cigarette smoke. There are some of smokers who also intentionally smoke cigarettes in public places in order to make the smoke they breathe out could be inhaled by other people and make them get cancer disease.An activity that damages your body is a sinning so that cigarette can be categorized as a proscribed thing or object that needs to be avoided and kept away as far as possible. Clergies or religionists may have different perceptions to this issue.

I wish everything I have said can be contemplated by all of us so that the dangerous effects of cigarettes will not harm all of us. Do not get yourselves feel regret before too late. Forgive me for the mistake on my words during speech. Thank you for the attention.

Wassalamu’alaikum wr.wb

33. Pendapat tentang bahaya merokok dalam bahasa inggris


Kelas: XI
Mata Pelajaran: Bahasa Inggris
Materi: Writing
Kata kunci: Danger of Smoking

Pembahasan:

 

Smoking is very dangerous for our health. It is a major cause of lung cancer. Smoking is also linked to heart disease, stroke and other chronic lung diseases. Almost one third of deaths from coronary heart disease are attributable to smoking. Smoking can also increase your risk for cancer of the bladder, throat and mouth, kidneys, cervix and pancreas.

 

Smoking is dangerous both as first hand smoking (smoker itself) and second had smoking (people who lives near smokers).

 

What makes cigarettes so toxic and dangerous?

 

There are hundreds chemical components found in cigarette and its smoke which are harmful to human health. This includes tar, nicotine, and benzene.

 

Tars are several chemicals that are in tobacco smoke. It leaves a sticky, brown residue on your lungs, teeth and fingernails. It cuase black teetch and damage to your lungs.

 

Nicotine is a dangerous and highly addictive chemical. It can cause an increase in blood pressure, heart rate, flow of blood to the heart and a narrowing of the artery vessels that carry blood. Nicotine may also contribute to the hardening of the arterial walls, which in turn, may lead to a heart attack.

 

Meanwhile, Benzene can cause cancer, particularly leukemia, in humans.

 

Therefore, to protect our health we need to stop smoking and keep smokers away from public places.
