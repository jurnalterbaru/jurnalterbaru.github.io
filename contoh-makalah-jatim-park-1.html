5. deskripsi bahasa inggris jatim park 1 batu malang​


Jatim Park

East Java (Jatim) Park is located in Jl. Kartika 2 Batu offers recreation tourism of new entertainment amusement for all level. It begins from children, finite adolescent until grandmother and grandfather. Of course, this tourism object has drawn up as family tour area in East Java. To get to the location, is not too difficult, because, the object only 2, 5 kilos meters from Batu city. This tourism object is about 22 hectares width with 850 meters height to sea level, save the multifarious knowledge, and recreation area with various supporting facilities for having relax.

Jatim Park may have said as the different tourism object in East Java province, besides offering a recreation place and study center, we will get many kind new knowledge. Jatim Park has at least 36 kinds of facilities which able to be enjoyed visitor. Before enjoy all supporting facilities for recreation area, the visitors will find an interesting view of ‘Galeri Nusantara’. Then stepped into ‘Taman Sejarah’, which contained of miniature temple in East Java like Sumberawan temple (Singosari- Malang regency), customhouse of Kiai Hasan Besari Ponorogo and Sumberawan Statue.

The other facility which able to be enjoyed is ‘Agro Park’ presenting crop and rareness fruits, animal diorama, which consists of unique animals that have been conserved, supporting games (bowling, throw ball, scooter disco), Ken Dedes and Ken Arok food centre, etc.

Jatim Park is suitable for family recreation. The recreation area sites here offer unforgettable tour and can used as alternative media of study for your children

Jatim Park 1 or East Java Park 1 is one of the mainstays recreational parks of Malang city that managed to attract the city and city tourists, because the success of East Java Park 2 also has been built also not far from Taman Jatim 1. Taman Jatim is a concept of recreational rides combined with education, so that children can play and learn at the same time.

Anything in Jatim Park 1?

Area play in East Java Park area, in which there are a total of 53 rides of the game that you can enjoy, but it is also supported by public facilities such as restaurants, cafes, toilets, mosque until the parking lot is large enough.

Entrance fee of Jatim Park 1 :

Tickets sold in East Java Park belong to several classes: Entrance Ticket Jatim Park 1: Rp 70.000 for person for weekdays, while for weekend Rp 100.000.

Entrance tickets Jatim Park 1: Rp 130.000 per person for weekdays, while the weekend Rp 150.000, excess this ticket is inclusive entrance ticket to visit Museum Body

Ticket Sakti, which already include tickets Jatim Park 1, Batu Secret Zoo, Wildlife Museum, Body Museum, Eco Green Park, Predator Fun Park, Museum of Transport (D'mask + Runway 27) and BNS, HTM this ticket valid for 2 days is Rp 375.000, while valid for 3 days (Super Sakti Ticket) Rp 450.000

Student-saving tickets, suitable for students who will visit on weekdays, start at Rp 70,000 including one of 3 places: Body Museum, Eco Green Park or Predator Fun Park. There is also a student-saving ticket with a price of Rp 100,000 includes 3 such places

Terms
1. Children with height above 85 cm subject to full ticket [under 85 cm FREE]
2. Countless Weekday [monday-Thursday]
3. Weekend counted [Friday-Sunday, red date and high season]
4. Ticket includes Insurance
5. For entourage purchase 30 tickets Free 1 ticket valid for multiples & nbsp; (all types of tickets: regular / shock / magic)
6. Ticket prices include free rides games except gokart, trampoline, flying fox, games room and swimming equipment.
