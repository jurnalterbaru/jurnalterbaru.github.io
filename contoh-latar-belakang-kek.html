36. contohnya kek gimna ya ka:(​


[tex]{\bold{Answers :}}[/tex]

Future Simple :

(+) She will go to cinema tomorrow. (–) She will not go to cinema tomorrow.(?) Will she go to cinema tomorrow?

Simple Past :

(+) He ate food at fancy restaurant yesterday. (–) He did not eat food at fancy restaurant yesterday.(?) Did he eat food at restaurant yesterday?

Present Perfect :

(+) They have done the homework.(–) They have not done the homework.(?) Have they done the homework?

[tex]{\bold{Explanation :}}[/tex]

Future Simple :

Digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang akan terjadi/dilakukan.

Form :

(+) S + will + V1 + O/C(–) S + won't + V1 + O/C(?) Will + S + V1 + O/C

Keterangan :

S = Subject ; I you, they, we, she, he, it. won't = will notV1 = infinitive, e. g : eat sit, go, etc. O = Object (Objek) C = Complement (Keterangan)

Simple Past :

Digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang terjadi di masa lalu.

Form :

(+) S + V2 + O/C(–) S + did + not + V1 + O/C(?) Did + S + V1 + O/C + ?

Keterangan :

V2 = Past Form, e. g : ate, drew, saw, etc.

Present Perfect :

Digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang sudah/belum terjadi /dilakukan di masa lalu. Digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang sudah terjadi berulang kali. Digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang terdapat kata "since" (sejak).

Form :

(+) S + have/has + V3 + O/C(–) S + have/has + not + V3 + O/C(?) Have/has + S + V3 + O/C
