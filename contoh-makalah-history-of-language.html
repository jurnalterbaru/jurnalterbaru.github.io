28. write narrative text,about the history of G 30 S PKI,The history of indonesia​


Jawaban:     September 30th Movement, group of Indonesian military personnel who captured and murdered six generals in 1965, marking the commencement of the abortive coup that led to the fall from power of Sukarno, Indonesia’s first president.Late in the evening on Sept. 30, 1965, a group of army conspirators calling itself the September 30th Movement gathered in Jakarta with the aim of kidnapping and killing seven army generals in the early hours of the following morning. By dawn on Oct. 1, six of the generals were dead; the seventh, Abdul Nasution, had escaped. Later that morning the movement announced that it had seized power to forestall a coup against the president by a council of generals. In the meantime, General Suharto, commander of the army’s strategic reserve, began to gather the reins of power into his own hands. By evening he had seized the initiative from the conspirators.

The Indonesian Communist Party (Partai Komunis Indonesia; PKI) maintained that the coup attempt was an internal affair of the army. The army leadership, on the contrary, insisted that it was part of a PKI plot to seize power and subsequently embarked on a mission to purge the country of the perceived communist threat. In the following month the military slaughtered communists and alleged communists across Java and in Bali; estimates of the number of people killed ranged from 80,000 to more than 1,000,000. In the following years communists, alleged communists, and their families were frequently denied basic rights (e.g., the right to a fair trial, the right to equal opportunity in employment, and freedom from discrimination). Between 1969 and 1980, approximately 10,000 persons, primarily known or purported communists, were detained without trial on the island of Buru in the Moluccas.With the destruction of the PKI, one of the elements of balance that had supported the Sukarno regime was eliminated, and the president himself came under increasing pressure. In March 1966, against a background of student action, the army forced Sukarno to delegate extensive powers to Suharto, now chief of staff of the army. With his new authority, Suharto banned the PKI and moved gradually to consolidate his position as the effective head of government. In March 1967 the Indonesian legislature installed Suharto as acting president, and in March 1968 he was appointed to the presidency in his own right. Sukarno was kept under house arrest until his death on June 21, 1970.The years between 1965 and 1968 were among the most tumultuous and violent in Indonesia’s history, and this period has served as the backdrop for a number of widely acclaimed works of literature and film. Most notably, many of the short stories and novels of Pramoedya Ananta Toer—who was among those imprisoned in the Moluccas (for nearly 15 years)—poignantly portray the tensions that agitated Indonesian society prior to the failed coup, while his book Nyanyi Sunyi Seorang Bisu (1995; The Mute’s Soliloquy) specifically addresses his years on Buru. The events surrounding the September 30th Movement also provided the setting for the award-winning films The Year of Living Dangerously (1982) and Gie (2005).

Once in Java, there was a poor widow named Mbok Srini who lived alone in her modest house on the edge of the forest. He felt very lonely and prayed to the gods to bless him with a child. One night in his dream, he imagined something wrapped under a jungle tree. He considers this dream as a sign, the answer to his prayer. He then went to the forest to look for something wrapped in a cloth under a tree, he hoped to find a baby to care for. Finally he found an object wrapped in cloth under a tree just like his vision in his dream. When he unwrapped the wrapper, there was no baby in the cloth wrapped but only cucumber seeds. Suddenly he heard a terrible laugh; a green-skinned giant named Butho Ijo (in Javanese, meaning "Green Giant") appeared behind him. The green giant told him to plant cucumber seeds and he would have children. He had to take care of the child, but when the child grew up, Mbok Srini had to return the child to Butho Ijo. The giant wants to eat the child. Because she wanted to have children, Mbok Srini agreed with the Butho Ijo agreement.

Mbok Srini returned to her house and planted cucumber seeds in her backyard garden. Then, a golden golden cucumber grew from its seeds, and when Mbok Srini took the cucumber and opened it, a beautiful baby girl appeared in it. So, he named the baby girl Timun Mas which means "golden cucumber". Year after year, Timun Mas grows into a beautiful girl. He is a loving, kind, and diligent child who is always willing to help and care for the elderly Mbok Srini. Just a week before Timun Mas's 17th birthday, Butho Ijo appeared in front of Mbok Srini's house and reminded him of his promise, and announced that in one week he would return to collect Timun Mas. Mbok Srini is very sad and afraid of the terrible fate that awaits her beloved child. Mbok Srini heard that there was a strong rishi on a nearby mountain. He hurriedly went to the mountain to seek help from a wise hermit who lived in the mountain. After hearing the story, the old hermit gave him four small cloth bags with something in them. The four items in the bag are cucumber seeds, needles, salt and shrimp paste (shrimp paste). The hermit asked Mbok Srini to give these items to Timun Mas and told him to throw them when he was chased by a giant. Mbok Srini returned home and told Timun Mas what to do if the green giant chased him.

Finally on Timun Mas's 17th birthday, Butho Ijo appeared at Mbok Srini's house to take Timun Mas as promised: he would eat the girl. Mbok Srini ordered Timun Mas to run for his life. Timun Mas was really scared and scared and ran as fast as he could to try to escape from the giant. The giant was furious and destroyed Mbok Srini's house while chasing Timun Mas. Finally, Butho Ijo overtook Timun Mas who ran away. In his distress, Timun Mas opened one of his four cloth bags spreading cucumber seeds behind him. Suddenly a large cucumber vine appeared and choked the giant's body, trapping him so he could not move. This gave Timun Mas time to escape further. But the powerful giant finally managed to free himself and continue his pursuit. Timun Mas opened his second bag and spread the needle behind him. Suddenly the needle turned into a bamboo forest with a sharp tip that wounded the giant. The giant was badly injured trapped with sharp bamboo, but he made it through the sharp bamboo forest and chased Timun Mas who was running again. He opened his third bag and sprinkled salt behind him. Suddenly a sea appeared behind him to drown the evil giant. But the giant managed to swim through the sea and kept chasing him. Timun Mas, once again, almost caught and opened his last bag. He threw the shrimp paste behind him and continued to run desperately. Shrimp paste suddenly turned into a sea of boiling volcanic mud. Butho Ijo trapped in hot mud that boils, sinks and dies. Timun Mas finally survived and returned to his mother Mbok Srini, and they lived happily ever after.

36. contoh language features of explanation text dan penjelasan​


Explanation Text

JAWABAN:

The language features of explanation text:  

The using of Simple Present Tense

Simple present tense digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta /kebenaran umum (general truth), kebiasaan (habitual action), atau kejadian yang terjadi pada saat ini (present).  

Perhatikan bentuk present tense pada teks berikut ini!

Rain is the primary source of fresh water for most areas of the world. It is also providing suitable conditions for diverse ecosystems. In addition, rain water is also for for hydroelectric power as well as plants and crop irrigation. Rain provides us life. However, how does rain happen?

The using of Action Verbs  

Action verb adalah kata kerja (verb) yang menunjukkan adanya suatu aksi atau perbuatan.

Perhatikan action verb pada teks berikut ini!    

The phenomenon of rain is actually a water circle. The concept of the water cycle involves the sun heating the Earth’s surface water and causing the surface water to evaporate. The water vapor then rises into the Earth’s atmosphere. The water in the atmosphere cools and condenses into liquid droplets. The droplets grow until they are heavy and fall to the earth.

The using of Passive Voices  

Passive voice merupakan suatu bentuk kalimat dimana subjek kalimat menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Tidak seperti active voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

Perhatikan bentuk passive pada teks berikut ini!

When the droplets fall to the earth, it is called rain. The rain can be in the form of water or snow. However, not all rain reaches the surface. Some evaporates while falling through dry air. This is called virga, a phenomenon which is often seen in hot, dry desert regions.

The using of Noun Phrases  

Noun Phrases merupakan frasa yang berhubungan dengan noun atau kata benda.

Perhatikan noun phrase pada teks berikut ini!

Rain is the primary source of fresh water for most areas of the world. It is also providing suitable conditions for diverse ecosystems. In addition, rain water is also for for hydroelectric power as well as plants and crop irrigation. Rain provides us life. However, how does rain happen?

The using of Technical Terms  

Technical Terms merupakan istilah teknis.

Perhatikan technical terms pada teks berikut ini!

In addition, rain water is also for for hydroelectric power as well as plants and crop irrigation. Some evaporates while falling through dry air. This is called virga, a phenomenon which is often seen in hot, dry desert regions.

PEMBAHASAN:

Explanation text merupakan salah satu jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris yang menjelaskan mengenai suatu proses. Berbicara mengenai proses, teks ini hampir sama dengan procedure text. Namun, jika procedure text menjelaskan cara melakukan sesuatu (proses), teks ini menjelaskan bagaimana suatu proses tersebut terjadi. Dengan begitu, dapat kita ketahui bahwa explanation text berfungsi untuk memberikan informasi kepada pembaca tentang suatu proses tersebut.
